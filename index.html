What is the SDET ?

The Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET) is responsible for a set of activities focused on confidence that the requirements will be met and the product is being developed in accordance with them, through requirements gathering and thus able to write test plans, prepare and prepare test data. Among his functions he can be responsible for creating tests using tools and also frameworks modified by other peers. Besides, of course, developing automated testing tools and frameworks to do your job.

The Tech Skills

  • Programming
  • Designing
  • Testing methodology
  • Knowledge about Automation
  • Agile and DevOps exposure
  • Exposure to BDD (Behavior Driven Development)
  • Understanding of OOD (Object-Oriented Design)

The Soft Skills

  • Organization and Time Management skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Attitude to upgrade yourself
  • Passion/Interest
